FAQ's:

Why Decreps?

Here at Decreeps we aim to provide our customers with the best quality of food and service. All the items prepared here are freshly cooked and the ingredients are homegrown.

How do I place an order?

Pick any item you like, add your quantity and click add to cart and the proceed further to add your adddress and more details.

Payment and Shipping

We Only Provide Cash ON Delivery (COD) as of now. We are working on other payment methods.

How do I track my order

Currently we dont provide any tracking facility, but we are working on integrating map-tracking facility to our website.

Returns and Refunds

You cannot cancel a order after 5 minutes of ordering. However if you cancel it within 5 minutes, you wont need to pay anything since we provide COD.

Data and Privacy

We never share our users profile details with anyone.

How to delete your account?

After you login,go to your profile section.From there click on 'delete my account' option.Your account will be deleted.

Are the food items same as the illustrations provied on website?

Yes,all the illustrations provided are the actual photographs of our menu.Though the delivered items may vary a bit because of parcel packaging.
